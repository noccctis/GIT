Elian stood at the cliff’s edge, clutching the old lantern his grandmother had given him. The Festival of Lights blazed in the valley below, lanterns rising like sparks into the star-filled sky. Yet his lantern was different—its glass pulsed faintly, as though a star lived inside it. Tonight, he whispered, would be the night he discovered the truth of the legend.

The lantern flared

, and

before him unfolded a staircase of light

climbing into the heavens. Trembling, Elian stepped forward. Above, wings shimmered like silver fire. The gates of Caelum awaited, and the city above the stars called his name.
